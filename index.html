Statement

I started bringing a real camera on trips instead of just a phone, and it changed how I travel — slower, more attentive, always looking for the frame before the moment passes.

This site is where that habit lives. Every roll gets the same treatment: same warm grade, same square crop, so a market in Addis Ababa and a coastline in Ica sit in the same visual language. No filters, no presets from someone else's trip — just a process I'm still refining, one country at a time.
